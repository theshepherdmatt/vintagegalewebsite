What to Expect

When you contact us, we'll ask for photos of your speakers and details about their condition. This helps us provide an accurate quote and timeline.

Typical restoration timeline: 4-8 weeks, depending on the extent of work required and parts availability.

What we need from you:

  • Clear photos of the speakers (cabinets, drivers, hardware)
  • Description of any known issues or damage
  • Your location (for shipping/collection arrangements)
  • Your restoration goals and budget

Our Workshop

Located in the UK, our workshop is equipped with the tools and test equipment necessary to properly restore Gale loudspeakers to original factory specification.

We work on a limited number of restorations at a time, ensuring each project receives the attention it deserves. Quality over quantity has always been the Gale way.
