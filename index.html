The Gale Legacy

Founded in the 1970s at 23 Bruton Place in Mayfair, Gale Electronics was the vision of Ira Gale, Jon Bannenberg, Billy Woodman, and David Lyth. Together, they created some of the most iconic British hi-fi equipment ever made.

The GS401 loudspeaker and GT2101 turntable became benchmarks of engineering excellence, combining avant-garde industrial design with uncompromising acoustic performance.

Our Restoration Philosophy

Every restoration begins with a thorough assessment. We document the condition of each speaker, identifying worn components, driver damage, and cabinet issues. Our goal is always to preserve the original design intent.

From cabinet refinishing to crossover rebuilding, we use period-correct methods and materials. Each step is documented, each component carefully handled. The result is a speaker that performs exactly as it did when it left Bruton Place.
